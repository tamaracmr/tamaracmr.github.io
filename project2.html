Book Collection.

Collection of books by the writer Gabriel García Márquez. The collection has five books, where both the creation of different covers and the internal layout of these books were discussed. The concept for its realization was "magical realism", which the author deals with in all his books, and therefore an aesthetic based on it was performed.
