App Redesign – Tally Weijl


This app redesign project focused on creating a cleaner, more minimal and user-centered interface aligned with the brand’s visual identity. The layout and visual hierarchy were refined by removing unnecessary elements and improving spacing, typography and content structure.
Special attention was given to usability and navigation in order to provide a smoother, more intuitive shopping experience. The result is a modern, clear and visually consistent interface that enhances both user experience and brand perception.
