Feeling like a child again

Rayuela's name. Yesterday's games, comes from the classic hopscotch game, in which every child has enjoyed in his childhood. I present the logo with a kind, funny and retro look. The game of the position of the quadratines comes from a hopscotch stamped with stamps.
