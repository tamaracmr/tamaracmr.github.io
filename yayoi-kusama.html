Dynamic Interface Design


This project translates the essence of Yayoi Kusama’s work into an immersive digital interface. Through repetitive patterns, bold contrasts, and an intuitive structure, the design reflects Kusama’s fascination with infinity and self-expression.
The user experience is crafted to be seamless, allowing smooth navigation while immersing visitors in a visually striking environment that uniquely blends art and functionality.
